BAC Clones DAS Source For M34 Mouse Assembly

The BAC Clones.

End read sequences have been mapped to the M34 Mouse assembly using SSAHA. Clones positioned by appropriate read end pairs are indictated by gold rectangles with black outlines.

Fingerprinting maps allow the positioning of extra clones (using map2gp). These are indicated by gold rectangles without outlines. These may be associated (depicted with horizontal line) with single SSAHA mapped read ends (which are depicted as narrower black rectangles).
